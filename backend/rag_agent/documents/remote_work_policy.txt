Company Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. REMOTE WORK ELIGIBILITY
All full-time employees are eligible to work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Part-time employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week.
Remote work must be approved by your direct manager.

2. CORE HOURS REQUIREMENT
All remote workers must be available during core business hours:
- Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (local time)
- Must respond to messages within 30 minutes during core hours
- Required to attend all scheduled meetings (virtual or in-person)

3. EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY OF Zaman Bank
The company provides:
- Laptop computer
- Monitor (upon request)
- Necessary software licenses
- VPN access for secure connection

Employees are responsible for:
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Appropriate workspace
- Data security and privacy

4. COMMUNICATION EXPECTATIONS
- Daily check-in with team via Slack or Microsoft Teams
- Weekly 1-on-1 with direct manager
- Bi-weekly team meetings (mandatory attendance)
- Response time: Within 4 hours for emails, 30 minutes for urgent messages

5. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
Remote work is a privilege that may be revoked if:
- Performance metrics decline
- Communication becomes inconsistent
- Deadlines are frequently missed
- Team collaboration suffers

6. IN-OFFICE REQUIREMENTS
Employees must come to the office for:
- All-hands meetings (monthly)
- Department-wide events
- Client presentations (as needed)
- Team building activities (quarterly)

7. TRAVEL POLICY INTEGRATION
Remote work days do not count as travel days.
For business travel, standard travel policy applies.

For questions, contact HR at hr@company.com
